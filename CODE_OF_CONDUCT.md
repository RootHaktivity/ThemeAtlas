# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free project experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the project and community

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or hateful language
- Personal attacks or discriminatory remarks
- Publishing private information without permission
- Disruptive behavior that undermines collaboration

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.
